## 七、游戏世界观与背景设定

### 7.1 世界观设定（用于 AI 生成时的上下文）

```
世界观：一个融合中世纪欧洲与古代中国的架空村庄——"月牙村"。白天是宁静的田园村落，夜晚则被狼人的诅咒笼罩。村庄中有一位古老的预言家守护真相，一位隐居的女巫掌握生死之药，一位退伍猎人身怀绝技。狼人化身为村民混迹其中，每当满月之夜便露出獠牙。村民们必须在白天的集会中辨别真伪，投票驱逐狼人，否则村庄将在三日内覆灭。

美术风格：暗黑哥特+东方水墨，色调以深蓝、暗紫、血红为主，辅以金色点缀（代表正义/警长）。建筑融合哥特尖顶与中式飞檐。角色设计偏写实，带有奇幻元素。
```

### 7.2 场景故事板（用于背景图生成的额外上下文）

**开局场景描述**
```
The village of Crescent Moon gathers at the ancient round table under the Great Tree. Twelve seats, each marked with a mysterious rune. A heavy mist rolls in as the village elder announces that wolves have infiltrated their ranks. Torches flicker, casting long shadows. The full moon rises ominously behind the Gothic-Chinese fusion bell tower.
```

```
Dawn breaks over Crescent Moon village. The last wolf lies defeated, reverting to human form. Villagers embrace in relief. The seer's crystal dims, her duty fulfilled. Golden sunlight floods the cobblestone square. Cherry blossoms drift on a warm breeze. Peace returns.
```

```
A blood moon hangs over the ruined village of Crescent Moon. The surviving wolves howl in triumph atop the bell tower. The village square is empty and silent. Claw marks scar the ancient round table. Mist engulfs the last flickering torch as darkness claims victory.
```

## 八、生成工具推荐

| 素材类型 | 推荐工具 | 备注 |
|----------|----------|------|
| 角色立绘 | Midjourney / Stable Diffusion XL | 加 `--style raw` 保持一致性 |
| 背景图 | Midjourney / DALL-E 3 | 宽幅 `--ar 16:9` |
| UI 图标 | Midjourney + Rembg 去背 | 或 game-icons.net 开源 |
| 动画帧 | Stable Diffusion + AnimateDiff | 或手动 sprite sheet |
| 音效 | ElevenLabs SFX / Stable Audio | 短片段最佳 |
| 背景音乐 | Suno / Udio | 指定时长和循环点 |
